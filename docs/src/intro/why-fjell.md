# Why Fjell?

Fjell is for operators who need to answer "what is running, who authorized it, and how do I recover?" for every node in their fleet.

Three primary archetypes:

- **A1 Industrial gateway** — long-lived, regulated, change-controlled.
- **A2 Sensor/edge fleet node** — offline-first, attested remotely.
- **A3 Regulated field device** — compliance-auditable evidence chain.
